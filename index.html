Why Spendium

Built for your money, not for our metrics.

Everything you record lives in an on-device SQLite database. No server holds your spending. No SDK reports your behaviour. The internet is optional.

Your data stays yours

No backend server. Everything you record lives in an encrypted on-device SQLite database — we literally cannot see your expenses.

Works fully offline

On the subway, on a flight, in the middle of nowhere — it just works. Every screen reads from local storage, every entry is saved locally first.

Sync on your cloud

Google Drive or OneDrive, app-scoped folder only (appDataFolder / approot). One hidden file in your account — we can’t see any of your other files.

Multi-currency with historical FX

Record in any currency. Historical rates from the open frankfurter.dev service convert everything using the rate in effect on the day of each transaction.

Powerful spending analysis

Three views into your money: a categories donut, a flat or grouped transactions list with search & filter, and an overview with sparkline plus stacked or line breakdown.

Any time period

Today, week, month, year, all-time, or a custom date range. Charts and totals re-compute instantly on device — no round-trip to a server.

Quick entry with calculator

Add an expense in three taps: amount → category → done. The number pad doubles as a calculator (+, −, ×, ÷) for splitting receipts on the spot.

15 languages

English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Slovak, Czech, Turkish, Ukrainian, Hindi, Vietnamese, and Chinese (Simplified).

Backup & restore

Export everything to a lossless JSON file or CSV (great for spreadsheets) straight from Settings. Restore on another device in one tap.

Privacy first — by design, not by promise.

No Firebase Analytics, no Sentry, no Mixpanel. No ads. No accounts. Only the INTERNET permission, used solely to reach OAuth and the public exchange-rate service when you actually need them.
